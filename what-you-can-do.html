What you can do with this degree/certificate

Built for how hiring really works.

Hiring is moving away from the paper degree. About 85% of employers now say they hire for skills — but they keep falling back on degrees, because they can't trust what a résumé claims. A BRASA credential is the signal they are missing: proof of what you can actually do, that anyone can verify in seconds.

  1. 1 Your record is the proof. Every course you complete is demonstrated, dated, verifiable skill. A BRASA degree shows what you can do, not just where you sat — exactly what skills-based hiring is looking for.
  2. 2 Verified in one scan. An employer scans your QR and confirms it instantly — no background-check delay, no doubt. When a quarter to a third of résumés are faked, a credential that can't be faked stands out.
  3. 3 It opens the door. Apply with a credential employers can trust, rise out of a stack of unverifiable résumés, and get credit for everything you have learned — whatever path earned it.
  4. 4 Anywhere you are. Any country, any phone, any language — you present the same trustworthy record as anyone else in the world.

Proof, not promises.

Employers are hiring for skills — they just need a record they can trust. A BRASA credential rises out of the stack of unverifiable résumés: verified in one scan, recognized anywhere you are. Proof of what you can actually do.
